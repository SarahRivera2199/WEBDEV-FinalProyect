Home Page

This is Navira's Home Page. Here you can find:

- Your Streak: view how many days you have been active consecutively.

- Your Recent Activity: from reviewing notes to doing practice test view the history of you activity on the app.

- Course Collection: Here you can organize your courses in any way you desire, by subject or maybe semester, its up to you.

- Priority Task: This section shows you the earliest coming tasks you have due.


Pomodoro Timers

Navira offers to timers to best enhance your study sessions using the pomodoro methond:

- The Full Moon timer is the users study timer

- The New Moon timer is the user's break timer

Additionally, this page offers a to-do list to help users keep track of their tasks during each session.
