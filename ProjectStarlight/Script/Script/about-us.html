Hello, My Name is Sarah Rivera!

My name is Sarah A. Rivera Ruiz. I'm a latina developer with roots in Mexico and Puerto Rico.

Much like the target audience of this application, I too am a student. I'm 3rd year university study, currently studying in the University Interamericana in Fajardo, Puerto Rico. I'm doing a bachelors in Computer Science


Navira - A Concept

I came up with the idea for Navira because I wanted to find the solution to my own problem, or at least thats how it started.

I like learning, but learning can be a bit difficult for me, so I rely on a lot of different methods to help me study. The problem is that a lot of these applications have their features hidden behind a paywalls or have limited features so I have to seek other applications for the study methonds I need. It's a hassle to have so many open tabs or applications, just trying to look for the proper tools available to me.

That's why I'm developing Navira, I believe that everyone should have easy access to the proper tools to help you progress academically.
